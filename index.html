Become a good web developer

Tips that help you avoid having to re-learn

Now, where doI start?

If you just decided to start your jorney to become a web developer, (yeayyyyy!!!), welcome to this amazing industry. A lot of us get too exited and sometimes start with the left foot. But in this article I want to help you to avoid the mistakes that I made when I started.

Now let's identify what are to common mistakes:

  1. Take your time to learn
  2. Understand the basics
  3. Master one skill at a time
  4. Think about an idea to start
  5. Get involved with the community
  6. Don't try to have a 100% perfect project
  7. Release what you have
  8. Feel proud about it
